SAHYADRI

About

Sahyadri College of Engineering & Management (SCEM), Mangalore was established in the year 200. It is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University and is approved by AICTE and Govt. of Karnataka. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate and research programs in the discipline of Engineering & Management.

SAHYADRI PLACEMENTS

About

Admission to SCEM depends on the performance of the candidates in various National/State entrance examinations. Furthermore, the college has a dedicated Training, Placement and Career Guidance Cell which provides 100% placement assistance to its students with the highest package offered is INR 40 LPA. Some of the top recruiters are Microsoft, Wipro, Infosys, IBM, SAP Lab, Cohesity, Adobe, JusPay, Zomato, BYJUs, SilverPeak Global etc.
